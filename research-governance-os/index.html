What belongs on this record

Submit an item when a decision, approval, prioritization, ownership assignment, or escalation is needed — or when the group must be made aware of something that affects governance, security, or spend.

This is not a status update forum. Items appear here only if the meeting must act on them.

How to submit

File the minimum first: a title, a topic type, and the decision needed — or the context the group must know. Your item lands as a Draft. Enrich the full record (context, options, recommendation, risks) by editing your draft any time until the agenda is published. New submissions close Wednesday at close of business; your existing draft stays editable after that.

Decision-required items must carry an Owner, a Decision needed statement, and a Due date before the agenda can be published. Flags on the agenda are advisory until publish.

Operating language

  • Decision needed — the single question the meeting must answer.
  • Owner — one accountable person, by name. Not a team.
  • Due date — when the decision or action is required, as a plain date.
  • Recommendation — the submitter's proposed answer, stated plainly.
  • Carry forward — unresolved at close; clones to the next meeting with lineage.
  • No decision required — awareness-only; timeboxed, never debated.
